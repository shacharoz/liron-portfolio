lectures

Educational game design | learning experiences design | game development

SIMILARITIES BETWEEN GAME DESIGN AND INSTRUCTIONAL DESIGN

Educational and instructional design could learn from good game design at how to tangle the users into the web of curiosity, challenge and fun. I show how these two design fields are actually quite similar.

DISSECTING THE GAME: 101 TO VIDEO GAMES

Why people love to play so much? Why players get so involved? Why do they play more than they watch TV? What happens inside the game that leaves the player with a feeling of "I want more"?

technologies from the movies: coming to life

survey of innovative technologies that were showed in TV series and movies that nowadays come to life. gesture recognition, speech recognition, health monitoring, robots

predicting player personality according to game behavior

can the player behavior inside the game predict what is the best job for them?

About

passion

Design junky. Tech savvy and super curious. Driven to reconnect learning with enjoyment and make products more accessible. Married to Liron - my partner, my home and my compass.

do Today

Senior user experience and game designer at Intel, specializing in motion tracking and gesture recognition.

Founded FLUX, an advisory firm for startup to build engaging mechanics for their products, and designing and developing joyful learning solutions.

Lectures on effective usage of games in education. An activist in the local design, development, gaming and edu-tech communities.

in the future

Develop a technology that would understand the user intentions and feelings. Use that data to create products that can help users get by more easily.

Design enjoyable learning experiences. Share my theory to use game and play in order to identify interests and teach specific types of learning matters.

oh, and also: take over the world!
